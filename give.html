Donate to the OK2PLAY Foundation

The OK2PLAY Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit providing free injury prevention and rehabilitation services to underserved high school athletes who have a path to college through sports. Your gifts make it possible for us to help more athletes. All donations are tax-deductible.

The OK2PLAY Foundation helps high school athletes reach their full potential by preventing and treating ACL tears, ankle sprains and other sports injuries.
